Wardrobe Makeovers for Women


As a Stylist, when I hear the phrase “I’ve got a wardrobe full of clothes and nothing to wear”, what my client really means is that their wardrobe is missing the essential items that form the base of all great outfits. So in order to fix this problem, I offer the best Wardrobe Makeover service I can provide.

I’ll teach you to look at your clothing and body in a new way, a way that focuses on positives and options. I’ll help you eliminate poor shopping choices, clothes that you rarely wear and slip you into something comfortable and stylish that feels like you.

No wardrobe is too big or small, and we will organize your wadrobe in two areas - BASICS and SEASONAL.

Once we have decided what pieces of clothing you want and what you don't want, this is a link where you can swap or donate your old clothes.



BASICS: These are the building blocks of your wardrobe, the essential styles that you wear all year.
SEASONAL: These are the styles appropriate for the different seasons in the year.

Identify Your Style

Based on the description and picture choose ONE lifestyle that matches your image.


Bohemian represents an alternative way of dressing, paired up with an equally alternative, more liberated lifestyle and a social stance against society’s constraints.
Classic style represents timeless looks, beautiful but simple cuts in clothing, and fine natural fabrics. Tradition is important and your clothing rarely goes out of style.
Casual wear emphasizes is on comfort, relaxation, and informality. Casual dressing places personal expression and comfort over formality and conformity.
The trendy and fashionable style is an embrace of the new trends each year. These are fresh off the runway looks are about taking risks and standing out.
